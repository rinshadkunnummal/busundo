# busundo
## Bus & Transport Tracker

A smart transportation module designed to help students plan their commute efficiently. By providing real-time information about campus buses, it minimizes waiting time and reduces uncertainty.

### Key Features

* **Live Bus Location** – View the real-time location of each campus bus on an interactive map.
* **Estimated Arrival Time (ETA)** – See when the bus will reach your stop.
* **Delay Notifications** – Receive instant alerts if a bus is delayed, cancelled, or rerouted.
* **Route & Stop Information** – Access complete bus routes, stop locations, and schedules.
* **Bus Occupancy (Optional)** – Check how crowded a bus is before boarding.
* **Favorite Routes** – Save frequently used routes for quick access.
* **Push Notifications** – Get reminders when your bus is approaching your selected stop.
* **Emergency Contact** – Quickly access transport office or driver contact information when available.

### Benefits

* Reduces unnecessary waiting time.
* Helps students plan their day more effectively.
* Improves the overall campus transportation experience.
* Encourages greater use of campus transport through reliable, real-time information.
